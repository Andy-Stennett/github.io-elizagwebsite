Ingredients
  • 250g tofu
  • Chickpea flour
  • Nutritional yeast
Method

  1. In a food processor blend tofu until smooth, 2 large table spoons of chick pea flour & continue to blend.

    Add 2/3 tablespoons of nutritional yeast and continue to blend.

  2. Add chickpea flour until the mixture resembles a dough and can easily be moulded into small ball which can be rolled out to make snake like shapes.

  3. Chop the dough and create small pocket like shapes.

  4. Add into boiling water and cook for around 15/20 minutes drain and top with a delicious italian style tomato sauce.

  5. Top with nutritional yeast and fresh Spinach.

Ingredients
  • 2 Frozen bananas
  • 1 Scoop any protein powder
  • 1 small banana
  • Frozen fruits ( I’ve used strawberries black current & redcurrants)
  • Dried coconut slices
Method

  1. Blend together frozen banana with protein power & combine in a high speed food processor or blender until smooth.

  2. Scoop into bowls and tops with Frozen fruits and dried coconut.
